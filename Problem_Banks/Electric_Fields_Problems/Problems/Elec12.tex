Consider an infinite slab of charge with a uniform volume charge density $\rho(\units{C/m^3})$
throughout its volume. The thick slab is located in a vertical (y,z) plane between $x = -L$ and $x = +L$.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Use Gauss’s Law to determine the Electric Field for any point $x > L$ to the right of the slab.
    \item Use Gauss’s Law to determine the Electric Field for any point inside the slab ($-L \leq x \leq +L$).
\end{enumerate}
